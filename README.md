# Tax Regime Calculator (India 🇮🇳)

## Overview

A web-based application that helps users compare the **Old and New Tax Regimes** and identify the most suitable option based on their income, investments, and deductions.

The goal is to simplify tax decision-making through a structured and user-friendly flow.

---

## Live Demo

- Live Application: [https://your-live-link-here](https://tax-calculator-orcin-eight.vercel.app/)

---

## Problem Statement

Tax planning in India is often complex due to:
- Multiple tax slabs and rules
- Various exemptions and deductions
- Difficulty in selecting the optimal tax regime

Users often struggle with clarity while making tax-related decisions.

---

## Solution

This application addresses the problem by:
- Collecting structured user financial inputs
- Evaluating tax scenarios
- Recommending the most suitable tax regime
- Presenting simplified explanations of key tax concepts

Based on the PRD, the application was built using an AI-assisted development approach.

- **GitHub**: Version control and source code management
- **Vercel**: Deployment and hosting

---

## Features

- Walkthrough of Old vs New Tax Regimes and various tax slabs  
- 10 guided questions (salary, investments, deductions, home loan, etc.)  
- Explanations of key tax components:
  - Section 80C
  - Rebate u/s 87A
  - Tax slabs
  - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
- Instant recommendation of the optimal tax regime
